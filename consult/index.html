Services

Here's (some) of what I do as a product manager:

Strategy

  • Decision framework development (how do you decide what to build?)
  • Competitive and market analysis, model-building.

Planning

  • Breaking projects into manageable, actionable pieces.
  • (Collaboratively with engineering teams) estimating project timelines.

Specification

  • Detailing requirements with exacting clarity (I believe strongly in "user stories").
  • Visually specifying interactions, interfaces, and processes.
  • Building interactive prototypes.

Project management

  • Managing iterative development and test processes.
  • Tracking progress towards milestones.

Measurement

  • Selecting and tracking meaningful success metrics.

Team building

  • Help to find and hire great product managers.
  • Coaching and mentoring staff product managers.

I love doing this job. I also love helping organizations develop their own expertise in product management, so I try to establish repeatable processes (templates, examples, training, etc.) My goal is to help you build great products over the long run.

Contact me for rates and availability. I offer discounted rates for social enterprises.
